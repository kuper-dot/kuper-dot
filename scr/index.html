Hello. My name is Anton

I'm a Ukrainian national who immigrated to the UK in 2023. During my childhood, my father sparked an interest in computers, and that passion has only grown. By now, I've decided my future career must be in the IT sector.

When my family and I first moved to the UK, I enrolled in the Information Technology Course at Bridgend College, where I completed BTEC Levels 2 and 3. My time there allowed me to gain a solid foundation in core IT skills.

Key Achievements

  • I competed in the Skills Excellence in Wales competition for the "IT Technician" discipline, where I proudly won a gold medal.
  • In November 2025, I was a competitor on the national level at World Skills UK in the same discipline. While I didn't win a medal there, I was highly commended for my performance.

What I Am Doing Now

I am currently a first-year Computer Science student at the University of South Wales, focusing on modules like Computer System Concepts and Information Management, Assurance and Security.

In my personal time, I actively manage a home lab consisting of a TrueNAS and a Proxmox virtualization system. This hands-on work has forced me to master practical skills like configuring DNS, managing networking protocols, and securing SMB shares using Linux ACLs.
